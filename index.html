About Me

I am a second-year Finance major in the Terry College of Business pursuing a minor in Computer Science and a FinTech certificate. I am obsessed with marrying financial information with technology and algorithms. I got my start with data pouring over the statistics on the backs of baseball cards as a kid and turned that love into founding my high school's Baseball Data & Analytics Program. Now, I am looking to utilize my love for data and analytics to help bridge the communication gap between IT and Financial teams to make numbers-based, people-first, fiscally-responsible business decisions. After undergrad, I am looking to pursue a Masters in Business Analytics to help seal the connection between my Finance and Computer Science coursework. Beyond technical skills, I am a people-first, relation-building person that loves creating solutions for people's needs.

NHL Weather API

A Java executable that uses the OpenWeather API to get the current weather for a the hometown of a user-selected NHL player. The program uses the NHL API to get information about the player, and then pipes the hometown of the player to the OpenWeather API to get the real-time weather for that location.
